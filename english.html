About company

The main activity of LLC Primary Wood Processing Enterprise is the manufacture of edged boardsThe range of products is represented by boards of all grades (A, B, C), natural moisture (22%) from coniferous trees (spruce, pine). By using modern equipment our qualified personnel carry out wood sawing according to the standard sizes specified in the Product range section. Edged board can be made according to your technical specifications.

Another focus area of our company is related to the supply of finished wood products. The warehouses of our partners contain the following products: euro-batten (DIN 68-126/86), imitation of timber (DIN 68-126/86), floorboard | grooved board (DIN 68-126/86), planed board (moisture 12% - 14%), beadboard (DIN 68-126/86), deck board (DIN 68-126/86), planken (DIN 68-126/86). We arrange delivery. You can check the availability of goods and the prices by contacting us via e-mail or Telegram.
