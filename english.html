About company

The main activity of LLC Primary Wood Processing Enterprise (PPPD in transliteration of the company's Russian name) is the manufacture of edged boards (DIN 68252 | GOST 8486-86). The range of products is represented by boards of all grades (A, B, C) from coniferous trees (spruce, pine). By using modern equipment our qualified personnel carry out wood sawing according to the standard sizes specified in the Product range section. Edged board can be made according to your technical specifications. After sawing, the timber is placed in a special timber-drying kiln where the drying process proceeds untill the moisture content reaches 10% - 14%.

Another focus area of our company is related to the supply of finished wood products. The warehouses of our partners contain the following products: euro-batten (DIN 68126-86 | GOST 8242-88), imitation of timber (DIN 68126-86 | GOST 8242-88), floorboard | grooved board (DIN 68126-86 | GOST 8242-88), planed board (moisture 10% - 14%), beadboard (DIN 68126-86 | GOST 8242-88), deck board (DIN 68126-86 | GOST 8242-88), planken (DIN 68126-86 | GOST 8242-88). We work with legal entities and individuals. We arrange delivery. You can check the availability of goods and the prices by contacting us via e-mail or Telegram.
