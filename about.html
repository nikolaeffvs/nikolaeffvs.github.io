Mathematical Salon

Welcome to our Mathematical Salon! Based in Saint-Petersburg, Russia. The objective of the Salon is to reduce bureaucracy and stimulate mathematical and cultural enrichment. Also, Salon holds seminar meetings on a weekly basis during the semester. The seminar has been established under the influence and as a consequence of the Real Analysis Symposium visited us in summer 2018.

If you would like to organize a meeting, give a talk or just join us feel free to contact us.
