The White Nights' Symposium
Summer Symposium in Real Analysis, June 9-15, 2018

The Forty-Second Annual Summer Symposium in Real Analysis has been held on June 9-15, 2018 at the Saint-Petersburg Department of V.A.Steklov Institute of Mathematics and Herzen University. The Symposium visited Russia for the first time!

On Conference

This conference has been held annually since 1978, twice in 1982, and is considered to be the premier conference of its type by members of the real analysis community. Initially stimulated by questions in differentiation theory posed by Andy Bruckner, the past three decades has seen a surge of work in real analysis. Many of the original questions have been solved, new programs of research arisen and applications of ideas first addressed in real analysis have found central positions in partial differential equations and in functional analysis.
